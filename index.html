leotools

We provide client-side data tools for working with online public speaking information. Specifically, we supplement or augment publicly available Toastmasters information. If you're technically minded, we can always use the help at github.

Our primary proof of concept is what is affectionately termed the LEO Board.

We are also experimenting with some high level views of district statistics - district-comparison.

Otherwise, here are some publicly available samples:
